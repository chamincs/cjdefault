KTC is Sri Lanka's one of private limited tennis centre . A brainchild of its three founding directors; Chamila, the academy aims to nurture the development of tennis in the country with a particular focus on quality of standards. The primary objectives of Hit Tennis include advanced training squads, coaches' education, school's Tennis 10s, tournaments and travelling teams of students.
